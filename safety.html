安全提醒

小心高溫燙傷。
Be careful of high-temperature burns.

小心割傷。
Be careful of cuts.

請戴上口罩。
Please wear a mask.

請戴上耳罩。
Please wear earmuffs.

請戴上護目鏡。
Please wear goggles.

請把頭髮綁起來。
Please tie up your hair.

請把袖子的扣子扣好。
Please make sure your sleeves are buttoned up.

請穿工作圍裙。
Please wear an apron.

工具勿放口袋。
Do not put tools in your pocket.

受傷時必須報告老師！
Report to the teacher when injured!

搬運較大或較重的物品時請小心。
Be careful when moving large or heavy items.

請勿在教室裡奔跑、嬉戲。
Do not run or play in the classroom.
